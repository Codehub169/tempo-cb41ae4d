Our Eye Care Services

Comprehensive solutions for your precious eyesight, delivered with expertise and care.

Cataract Surgery

We specialize in state-of-the-art phacoemulsification (micro-incision) cataract surgery. This procedure involves removing the cloudy natural lens and replacing it with an advanced intraocular lens (IOL), restoring clear vision with minimal discomfort and rapid recovery time.

Book a Consultation

LASIK & Refractive Surgery

Say goodbye to glasses and contact lenses. Our clinic offers a suite of refractive surgeries, including blade-free LASIK, PRK, and ICL, to correct myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. We use the latest laser technology for precision and safety.

Am I a Candidate?

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Children's eyes require special attention. Our dedicated pediatric ophthalmologists are experts in diagnosing and treating conditions like amblyopia (lazy eye), strabismus (crossed eyes), and refractive errors in a child-friendly environment.

Schedule a Check-up

Glaucoma Management

Known as the 'silent thief of sight,' glaucoma requires early detection and consistent management. We provide comprehensive glaucoma screening, diagnosis, and treatment options, including medication, laser therapy, and surgical procedures to preserve your vision.
